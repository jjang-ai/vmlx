What it is:

What it is:
The prefix cache is a higher-level optimization that stores complete KV cache states for frequently-repeated input sequences (prefixes). | KV Cache | Prefix Cache |
 Granularity | Per-token K and V vectors | Complete sequence states |
 Scope | Single generation session | Across multiple requests |
 Reuse | Within one sequence | Across different requests |
 Memory | Grows with sequence length | Grows with cached prefixes |
 Use Case | Enables autoregressive generation | Enables prefix sharing |
